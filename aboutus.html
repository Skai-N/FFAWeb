MISSION

Food for All NYC is a nonprofit organization that was created by Stuyvesant High School students with an aim to redistribute excess fresh foods from schools, grocery stores, and delis to those in need around New York City. We utilize food pantries, community meal centers, and community fridges around New York City as they help us push our vision of a healthier, more collaborative, and less wasteful city.
